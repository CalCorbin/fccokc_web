Join Us

freeCodeCamp Oklahoma is a group for those who want to learn to code throughout the Oklahoma state area.

Meetings

We stream virtual meetups the 2nd Sunday of each month on Twitch, along with other occasional in-person meetups in order to supplement the online learning with in-person guidance and instruction. We do our best to keep you up-to-date and moving forward in your career as web/software developers!
